# Inventory Operational Dashboard
### Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing inventory operations using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into inventory levels, demand forecasting, product movement efficiency, discount impact, and competitor pricing analysis.

The objective of this project is to support inventory optimization, identify stock imbalance, and improve operational decision-making through interactive visual analytics.

### Objectives
- Analyze inventory levels across categories and stores
- Compare stocked inventory with predicted demand
- Evaluate product movement efficiency
- Understand the impact of discounts on revenue
- Compare pricing gaps with competitor pricing
- Identify overstock and understock situations
- Enable interactive filtering for operational monitoring

### Tools & Technologies Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- CSV Dataset
### Created Interactive Dashboard with 2 pages one which gives Insights of Overview of Inventory and other operational Insights where both the dashboards are inteconected 

## Dashboard Features
1. Inventory Overview Dashboard

#### The overview page provides high-level operational KPIs including:

- Total Revenue
- Average Stock Level
- Total Units Sold
- Average Discount

Visuals Included:
- Sales comparison by category
- Revenue contribution by category
- Average stock level analysis
- Store-level operational metrics


2. Inventory Operational Dashboard
- #### Price Gap vs Competitor Price
   This visual compares internal pricing against competitor pricing to identify pricing gaps across categories.

-  #### Discount Impact Analysis
       A scatter/bubble chart was used to analyze the relationship between discounts and revenue generation.

  Key Insight:
   Despite higher discounts in certain categories, revenue remained relatively stable, indicating that discount strategies may have positively influenced sales volume without significantly impacting overall revenue.

-  #### Predicted Stock vs Stocked Inventory
  This chart compares:
- Demand Forecast
- Inventory Level
- Units Sold
  
Key Insight:
  Certain categories showed higher stocked inventory compared to predicted demand, indicating potential overstock situations.

- #### Product Movement Efficiency
      This matrix visual evaluates inventory turnover and movement efficiency across stores and categories.


##### Key Insights
- Clothing and Toys categories generated high revenue contribution.
- Inventory levels in some categories exceeded demand forecasts, indicating excess stock risk.
- Discounted products continued generating strong revenue performance.
- Product movement efficiency varied significantly across stores.
- Competitor pricing gaps were more noticeable in selected categories.
